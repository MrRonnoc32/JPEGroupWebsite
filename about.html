About JPEG

Learn more about our mission, team, values, and partnerships.

The Jacksonville Policy Engagement Group (JPEG) is a collective of policy professionals dedicated to improving policy outcomes for our community. We strive to empower all Jacksonville residents to be engaged in public policy through education, dialogue, and action.

Our Leadership Team

Rhianna Scyster

Rhianna's elevated experiences & passion for community service have developed Rhianna's expertise on the impact of empowering differences and advocating for equity. She is dedicated to improving Jacksonville through social justice, policy development, and servant leadership.

Outside of work, Rhianna enjoys spending time reading fiction, writing, and spending time with her daughter and husband.

Monique Stewart

Director of Development

Monique Stewart is a dynamic and results-oriented professional with extensive experience in strategic planning, leadership development, and community engagement.

Our Values and Principles

As public policy professionals, we aim to empower all Jacksonville residents to be engaged in Public Policy through education, dialogue, and action.

We believe that informed citizens, critical conversations, and coordinated community efforts are essential for creating equitable and sustainable outcomes for underrepresented and historically marginalized communities.
