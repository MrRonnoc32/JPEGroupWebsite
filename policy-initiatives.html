Policy Initiatives

Explore the issues JPEG is addressing in the community.

The Jacksonville Policy Engagement Group (JPEG) is a collective of policy professionals dedicated to improving policy outcomes for our community. We strive to empower all Jacksonville residents to be engaged in public policy through education, dialogue, and action.

Our current policy focus areas reflect a commitment to civic engagement at both the local and national levels. Hometown Huddle fosters community-based dialogue and grassroots organizing around pressing local issues. We maintain an active presence at City Council meetings to monitor legislation, provide public comment, and hold elected officials accountable. Our Capitol Hill visits allow us to engage directly with state policymakers and advocate for the interests of Jacksonville residents. Through Election Reflection, we analyze past election cycles to assess turnout trends, campaign effectiveness, and voter behavior. Finally, our initiatives around Voter Registration and voting guides aim to expand access to the ballot, especially among historically underrepresented communities, by providing clear, nonpartisan information and registration support.
