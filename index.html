Who We Are

The Jacksonville Policy Engagement Group or "JPEG" is a group of policy professionals dedicated to improving policy outcomes for our community.

Through community conversations on current events, monthly newsletters, and collaborative work sessions, we aim to create more equitable and sustainable outcomes for underrepresented and historically marginalized communities.

We believe that in partnership with you, education, dialogue, and advocacy are key factors in overcoming systemic barriers and this is just the beginning for us!
